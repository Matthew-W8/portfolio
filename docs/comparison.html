Comparison of 19th Century fiction and 19th Century Innagural Addresses

In my research, it seems that the 19th century fiction uses more negative words like "don't" or "couldn't" while the addresses use postive words like "will" and "may be". I think their is something to compare here between the negativity in the fiction and the postivity in the addresses which may reveal something about the time period.
